[green]KamAZ 4310 fixes [normal]v1.35 by Cheva

Suitable for any version of this KamAZ (1.30 and higher)
Placae this mod above original KamAZ-4310 mod in manager.

[blue]My fixes:[normal]
	- Sounds: filtered, added, changed interior and common sounds and noises.
	- Engines: now 400 HP and 700 HP are real 400 and 700, not 800 and 1500. 
	- The parameters of torque and speed are prescribed.
	- Fuel flow lowered too.
	- Used official data for Liebherr/KamAZ 2014 700HP and KamAZ 740.73 400HP.
	- Chassis: fuel capacity changed to 1200 liters (was 800) for 6x6.
